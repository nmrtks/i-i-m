Information

This 3D model is a simplified representation of the skeletal structure of the elbow joint and forearm. It illustrates how the three joints—the humeroulnar joint, the humeroradial joint, and the proximal radioulnar joint—contribute to elbow flexion and extension, as well as forearm pronation and supination.

Orbit : Left mouse
Pan : Right mouse
Zoom : Scroll
Flexion / Extension : Vertical scrollbar
Pronation / Supination : Horizontal scrollbar
